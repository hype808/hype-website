If you would like to support the work HYPE does, please consider donating. Donors can write a check directly to HYPE made payable to Hawaii Youth Percussion Ensemble and mailed to: 2279 Liliha Street, Honolulu, Hi 96817. This is the preferred method of donation.

As a recognized 501(c)(3) non-profit organization, all donations are tax deductible to the fullest extent of the law.

You can also donate to us online using the button below. However, please keep in mind that there is a processing fee of 5.9% + 30 cents taken from your donation. As such, mailing a check directly to HYPE is the preferred method of donation, as there is no fee involved.

Funds collected will go towards various percussion education opportunities such as:

  • rehearsals
  • private lessons
  • master classes
  • clinics
  • participation in national and international competitions and festivals

In addition, your funds will also support the following:

  • rehearsal and performance space rentals
  • sheet music purchases
  • insurance
  • event promotion
  • educational outreach
  • instrument and equipment repairs and purchases
