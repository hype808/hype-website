Additional Notes

Students must be in grades 6-12 to participate. Please contact us if there is an interest to participate if you are in grade 5 or lower.

If there is an interest to participate but there is a financial need, please contact us.

All checks are made payable to: Hawaii Youth Percussion Ensemble (memo line: student's name)

Students should bring their own mallets and sticks. Students may borrow sticks and mallet for an additional fee of $1 per day.

Regular concert attire: black dress shirt (short or long sleeve), black dress pants, black dress shoes and socks
